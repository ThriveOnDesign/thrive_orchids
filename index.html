We sell the most common type of orchid which is the Moth Orchid (phalaenopsis), they are much easier to take care of than other orchids and typically bloom for two to four months. The flowering time will depend on the condition of the plant and how well it is cared for. Orchids can rebloom 1-2 times per year and can live for many years, with some records of orchids living for over 100 years.
